What is a C-Arm Machine?

A C-Arm is a specialized X-ray imaging device used during surgical and orthopedic procedures. It provides real-time imaging, allowing doctors to see inside the patient’s body without invasive surgery.

Because of its importance in surgeries, even a minor malfunction can lead to serious delays and risks.

Common issues include:

  • Image distortion or poor quality
  • Power supply failure
  • Mechanical arm alignment problems
  • Software or calibration errors
  • Detector or X-ray tube issues
